Reservoir Inflow Prediction Using Hybrid Machine Learning

Sector: Water Resources & Hydrology

Problem: Reservoir operators needed reliable short- and long-term inflow forecasts to support release planning and flood risk reduction.

Solution: Civillytics developed a hybrid ML stack (XGBoost + ANN + SVM) trained on climate data, hydrological records, and multi-station weather inputs.

Results: Forecast accuracy improved by 27%, providing actionable insights for reservoir operations.

Construction Progress Dashboard with Automated S-Curves

Sector: Construction Management

Problem: Traditional reporting relied on Excel and manual updates, causing delays and inconsistent reporting.

Solution: We built a Python-powered workflow that automatically processes daily logs, generates S-Curves, compares Planned vs Actual, and publishes an online dashboard.

Results: Reporting time reduced from 2 hours per day to under 5 minutes. Complete transparency for project managers and clients.

Automated Laboratory Reporting for QA/QC Testing
