Who We Are

Infrastructure and environmental projects now rely on massive datasets—hydrological records, climate projections, GIS layers, laboratory outputs, project logs, and sensor streams. Yet most organizations still process this data manually.

Civillytics is a remote Civil Engineering × Data Science consultancy. We combine engineering domain knowledge with advanced analytics to build automated workflows, predictive models, and intelligent dashboards. We turn raw Excel files, climate data, and site records into clear, reliable insights that support better decisions.

Water & Environmental Data

We help hydrologists, researchers, and environmental consultants clean, visualize, and model complex datasets.

  • Climate Data Engineering: Cleaning and processing raw climate datasets (PMD, ERA5, GCM, satellite) for high-resolution analysis.
  • Hydrological Forecasting: Reservoir inflow prediction, flood forecasting, and time-series modeling using machine learning.
  • GIS & Remote Sensing: Watershed modeling, landcover classification, groundwater mapping, and spatial analytics.

AI & Machine Learning for Civil Engineering

We develop machine-learning models tailored for hydrology, water resources, and infrastructure systems. Our solutions are built by engineers who understand both the physics and the data.

  • Inflow Prediction Models: Short- and long-term reservoir inflow forecasting using hybrid ML techniques.

Quality Assurance (QA/QC)

We streamline workflows for Material Testing Laboratories, ensuring compliance with ASTM/BS standards.

  • Digital Lab Reporting: Convert raw machine outputs (compression tests, Marshall tests, sieve data) into automated PDF reports.
  • Statistical Compliance: Evaluate test variability, strength trends, and control charts for better decision-making.
  • Material Analytics: Soil, asphalt, and concrete data processing with automated grading and performance indicators.
